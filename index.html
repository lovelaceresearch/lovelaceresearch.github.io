Lovelace Research

Independent research-led innovation lab for personal & humane AI

Advancing the future of artificial intelligence through thoughtful research and design

We explore fundamental questions about AI systems, focusing on mental models, frameworks, and architectures that prioritize human values and personal alignment. Our work aims to create meaningful change in how we think about and build AI technologies.

About

Lovelace Research operates as a non-American PARC, infused with design thinking principles. We believe in fundamental change through rigorous research that ultimately translates into transformative products and paradigm shifts.

Our approach combines deep technical research with human-centered design, ensuring that advances in AI serve to augment and empower individuals rather than replace human agency.

Research Areas

Our interdisciplinary research spans multiple domains, each contributing to our vision of personal and humane AI.

Mental Models

Understanding how humans conceptualize and interact with AI systems to build more intuitive interfaces.

Frameworks

Developing theoretical and practical frameworks for evaluating and designing AI systems.

Futures

Exploring potential trajectories of AI development and their implications for society.

System Architecture

Designing AI architectures that prioritize transparency, control, and human agency.

Fine-tuning

Advancing techniques for personalizing AI models while maintaining safety and alignment.

Personal Model Alignment

Ensuring AI systems align with individual values and preferences without compromising broader safety.

Reasoning

Improving AI reasoning capabilities while maintaining interpretability and human oversight.

Our Approach

We believe that meaningful progress in AI requires a fundamental rethinking of current paradigms. Our methodology combines rigorous research with practical application.

  • Research-led innovation that prioritizes long-term impact over short-term gains
  • Design thinking principles applied to technical AI research
  • Focus on personal and humane AI that augments human capabilities
  • Interdisciplinary collaboration across technology, design, and social sciences
  • Open research culture that shares insights with the broader community
